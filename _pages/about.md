I'm a final-year BSc Computer Science & Engineering (Honours) student at TU Delft, with a track in Data & AI and a minor in Quantum Science & Quantum Information. I'm currently a student researcher at the [Infini Data Lab (WIS)](https://www.tudelft.nl) working on enabling fault-tolerant quantum computation through AI — specifically on efficient simulation of quantum systems using learning-based methods.

I've also built and shipped real systems in industry: at PwC I architected a full-stack deal analytics platform that replaced manual financial workflows and achieved 4× faster analysis (Python/Django, PostgreSQL, React, Docker).

I'm passionate about Maths, Physics, and ML, and I like working at the intersection of theory and real-world systems — turning complex ideas into things that actually run.

Outside of tech, I'm into chess (FIDE 1773), cricket (Dutch National U16 & U18 teams, #1 U17 bowler in the Netherlands in 2022), ancient history, and science fiction. I grew up in the Netherlands with Indian heritage and speak fluent English, Marathi, and Dutch, and am comfortable in Hindi and German.

Feel free to reach out if anything here resonates — always happy to connect.
